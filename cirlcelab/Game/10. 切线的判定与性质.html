切线的判定与性质
Tangent Line: Criterion & Properties
直线 ⊥ 半径 ⟺ 直线是切线
Line ⊥ radius ⟺ Line is tangent
📐 经过半径外端且垂直于该半径的直线是圆的切线；切线垂直于过切点的半径
📐 A line through the outer endpoint of a radius and perpendicular to it is tangent to the circle; a tangent is perpendicular to the radius at the point of tangency
🖱️ 拖拽点 A 改变切点，拖拽点 B 旋转直线Drag A to change tangent point, drag B to rotate the line
